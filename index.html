WHO WE ARE

A Space Built for Serious Learners

Intellect Library is Agra's dedicated self-study destination, located conveniently opposite St. Thomas Orthodox Church in Kahrai. We believe every student deserves a quiet, distraction-free environment to pursue their academic and professional goals.

Whether you're preparing for competitive exams like UPSC, SSC, banking, or simply need a focused space to study, our library provides everything you need — comfortable seating, proper lighting, a calm atmosphere, and a community of like-minded individuals.

We are open 7 days a week, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, with flexible membership plans to suit every schedule and budget.

WHAT WE OFFER

Our Facilities & Services

Everything you need for focused, productive study sessions — all under one roof.

Silent Study Zone

A strictly maintained silent environment so you can concentrate fully without any distractions.

Air Conditioned

Fully air-conditioned premises ensuring a comfortable temperature regardless of the season.

Ergonomic Seating

Comfortable study desks and chairs designed for long hours of productive work and reading.

Books & Resources

Access to a curated collection of books, magazines, newspapers, and study materials.

High-Speed Wi-Fi

Reliable internet connectivity for online research, digital learning, and accessing study portals.

Exam Preparation

Specially geared for students preparing for UPSC, SSC, banking, railway, and other competitive exams.

Locker Facility

Secure lockers available to keep your belongings safe while you study without worry.

Proper Lighting

Well-lit study environment with soft, eye-friendly lighting to reduce strain during long sessions.
